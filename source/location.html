Directions

Bricks on Central is located on East Central Avenue just east 3rd Street SE. Our neighbors are Ebeneezer's Eatery & Irish Pub, Firestone Tires and Cenex.

Parking

We have a large parking lot west of the building. There is plenty of street parking if the parking lot is ever full.
